Move and Zoom

Holding Spacebar, use the
hand tool to pan around. Zoom
controls are in the top right
corner.

FLOW CHART TOOLS

Stickies

Press “S” to quickly add a sticky
to the canvas.

Rectangles

Press “R” to quickly place a
rectangle onto the canvas. Other
shapes can be selected from the
toolbar or swapped from the
shape menu.

Stickers

Quickly access fun stickers to add to
your brainstorm. If you have component libraries in Figma, you
can also add them here. Find stickers and components in
the toolbox.
